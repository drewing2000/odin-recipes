Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2oz Woodford Reserve Bourdon
  • 1/2oz Maple Syrup
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Angostura Orange Bitters

Instructions

  • Add bourbon to chilled rocks glass
  • Add maple syrup
  • Add 3 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Add 2 dashes Angostura Orange Bitters
  • Carefully lower sphere of ice into glass
  • Stir and enjoy!
