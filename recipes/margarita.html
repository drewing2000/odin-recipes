Margarita

Ingredients

  • 2oz Camarena Reposado Tequila
  • 1/2oz Cointreau
  • 1/2oz Agave Syrup
  • 1oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1tsp Flake Salt

Instructions

  • Use wedge of lime to moisten half of chilled rocks glass
  • Roll wet rim in flake salt
  • Add tequila to glass
  • Add Cointreau to glass
  • Add agave syrup to glass
  • Add lime juice to glass
  • Carefully lower sphere of ice into glass
  • Stir and enjoy!
